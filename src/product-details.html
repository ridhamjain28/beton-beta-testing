Installation Philosophy

The Mag-Track system is engineered for recessed, surface, or suspended mounting. Our Gen II coupling ensures that modules remain securely locked while allowing for rapid reconfiguration by architects and designers without electrical expertise.

Tool-Free

All modules snap into place via high-density neodymium magnets.

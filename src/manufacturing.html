The Science of Light

At Beton Lighting, we believe lighting is more than just illumination—it is a technical discipline measured by precision metrics. Our engineering team focuses on the three pillars of visual performance:

lightbulb

CRI 95+ (Color Rendering Index)

Exceptional color accuracy ensuring reds are vibrant (R9 > 90) and skin tones remain natural under artificial light.

open_run

TM-30-18 Metrics

Advanced assessment of color fidelity and gamut, providing a comprehensive map of how light interacts with physical objects.

visibility

Low UGR (Unified Glare Rating)

Engineered optics designed for visual comfort, minimizing direct glare in workspace and gallery environments.

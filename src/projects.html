Flagship Case Study

The Obsidian Pavilion

The Obsidian Pavilion represents our most rigorous application of light as a structural element. By embedding high-output linear modules directly into the 12-ton concrete slabs, we achieve a seamless integration that challenges the boundary between fixture and architecture.

Materials: 80% Recycled Concrete, Blackened Steel, 3200K Linear LED Modules. Design Focus: Volumetric shadows and geometric precision.

Architectural lighting solutions designed for longevity, performance, and aesthetic purity. Based in India, illuminating the world.

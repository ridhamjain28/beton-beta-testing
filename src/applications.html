04 / Visual Focus

Retail

Retail lighting should reveal colour, texture, and product detail while guiding attention through the space.

Recommended: track lights, recessed spotlights, wall washers.

Consider: colour rendering, beam angle, display contrast, maintenance access.

05 / Productivity

Office

Office lighting needs visual comfort, uniformity, and efficient task illumination for long working hours.

Recommended: linear profiles, panel systems, low-glare downlights.

Consider: glare control, uniformity, desk layouts, energy efficiency.

06 / Reliability

Healthcare

Healthcare environments need dependable, comfortable lighting that supports clarity, movement, and task visibility.

Recommended: sealed luminaires, linear systems, task lighting.

Consider: visual comfort, reliability, cleanability, task zones.
